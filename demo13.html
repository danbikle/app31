app31/demo13

This page is a demo of /demo13.js which does the following:
  • Loads /csv/GSPC.csv into your browser
  • Cuts the CSV into 3 pieces:
  • | piece 1 | piece 2 | piece 3 |
  • It uses piece 2 to predict all observations (about 250) in piece 3.
  • It compares the predictions to the actual gains in piece 3.
  • The predictions are calculated using a model, /json/magicNet11.json, created by demo12.html
  • The JSON of the model (a lot of JSON) is displayed below the results.
I should use Inspect Element in my browser to watch this demo in the debugger.
