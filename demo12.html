This page is a demo of /demo12.js which does the following:
  • Loads /csv/GSPC.csv into your browser
  • Cuts the CSV into 3 pieces:
  • | piece 1 | piece 2 | piece 3 |
  • It uses piece 2 (the 'training piece') to create a model from 252*20 observations (20 years).
  • It converts the model to JSON.
  • The JSON of the model (a lot of JSON) is displayed below.
